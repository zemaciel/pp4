Welcome

The 4th Fork: Galways's Modern Gem

Welcome to The 4th Fork, a modern and sophisticated restaurant located in the heart of Galway City. Our laid-back atmosphere and stylish decor make it the perfect place for a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner with colleagues. Our menu features fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and a variety of dishes to satisfy any palate. Whether you're in the mood for a light salad or a hearty steak, our chefs will prepare it to perfection. Come visit us at Olive & Oak and experience the perfect blend of casual elegance and culinary excellence.
